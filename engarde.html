Rules

  • * Input four actions and they will play out simultaneously.
  • * Try to anticipate your opponents moves.
  • * Lose a round and your banner will be torn.
  • * Lose three and it will all be over.

Controls

  • Player One
  • Movement Keys - W, A, S, D
  • Attacking Keys - Q to Slash - E to Jab

  • Player Two
  • Movement Keys - P, L, ;, '
  • Attacking Keys - O to Slash - [ to Jab

  • 1, 2 - Toggles AI for that player.
